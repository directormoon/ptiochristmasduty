PTIO: Christmas Duty

Piggy: The Insolence Origins
Launch Date: TBD 2026

Synopsis

On Christmas Eve, you arrive in the quiet, snow-covered village of Polosville. What begins as a peaceful visit quickly unravels when Santa’s sleigh vanishes without a trace.

The task seems simple—recover it before Christmas is lost. But whispers lead you to Frostbite Mountain, a place no one dares to approach after nightfall.

To get there, you must survive the storm… and something that hunts you along the way. It never rushes, never looks away—always watching its prey. And whatever guards the fallen sleigh has no intention of letting you leave.

Gameplay Features

  • The danger increased In this chapter, you'll see unique and more dangerous gameplay... you'll never stop being watched by it.
  • Cooperation is more essential than ever With new features like being knocked down by Piggy, the urgency of teamwork is even greater; help each other, and everyone will manage to escape the hunt in time.
  • Piggy now acts differently Playing this chapter, you'll notice that you and Piggy are now much more evenly matched in score. You can still run like in PTIO: Reborn, but Piggy can too...
  • Don't make a mistake... it could cost you dearly... You no longer die instantly to Piggy, but be careful, even though you can be revived by your teammates, you become weaker in your "second life," you become slower and have less health, so make the most of what you have.
